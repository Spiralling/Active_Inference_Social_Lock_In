## The Thesis (Working Form)

Values function as priors in the agent's generative model (the C-distribution in active inference). Priors shape the precision-weighting of sensory channels — i.e., what is salient. Salience determines what gets attended to, copied, and amplified in a community. Communities therefore reproduce themselves through a self-referential loop:

> community prior → salience landscape → attention/copying → installation of prior in next generation

Norms, on this account, are stabilized regions of high-precision shared prior preference. Power (Bourdieu, Lukes' third face) is the asymmetric capacity to shape that precision allocation. AI-mediated information environments are a new locus of precision-allocation that can capture the loop.
