\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}
% =====================================================================

The technical contribution of this paper is to articulate norms as
salience priors whose multi-agent dynamics are governed by two
precision-related quantities --- a learned channel precision
$\trust{i}{j}$ and a variational cost of mind-change $\stub$ --- and
to derive the dissociation from Bayesian rule-induction stated in
Proposition~\ref{prop:limit}. A fuller discussion of the empirical
phenomena and political-theoretic implications is deferred to the
companion treatment.
